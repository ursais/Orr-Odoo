Accounting Analytic/Project Budget Management

This app allow you to manage your company income/expense budget on top of your analytic accounts.

Main Features:

  • Allow you to create budget by dates.
  • Budgetary Positions setup for income/expense budget types.
  • Allow you to planned your budget and compare it with Actual spending/earnings.
  • Linked budget position with real accounts to view budget practical amounts.
